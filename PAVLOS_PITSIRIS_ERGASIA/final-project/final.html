Pavlo Napa Beach Hotel: Where Quality and Care Combine

Pavlos napa beach hotel is a family-owned hotel that was build in 1989. Our hospitality journey starts at 1972 when our Grandfather Pavlos Tsokkos created a restaurant at the land that he used to grow orange trees as a farmer. The restaurant was a big success because people from all over the world experienced the Cyprus hospitality first handed and tasted comfort homemade food prepared with love from our Grandmother Chrystalleni Tsokkou. As the tourism rapidly increased the next years, the decision to build a hotel was a natural one. Science then Pavlo Napa strives to provide to our quests a quality tourism experience driven by the pure values our ancestor gave us.


The Pavlo Napa Beach Hotel consists of two buildings. All 147 rooms (side sea view and inland view) have been completely refurbished and offer all the comforts of a 4 star resort. All rooms are decorated in contemporary style with light textures and modern furniture. A big renovation is coming the next years, you can check the renewals in the renovation option in the menu.


Pavlo napa is situated in a prime location in Ayia Napa, one of the most popular resorts in Europe. The hotel has direct access onto the beautiful sandy bay (blue flag awarded) ,overlooking the crystal blue waters of the Mediterranean Sea, where tranquility and beauty are in perfect harmony with nature. Many activities and attractions are within walking distance from the hotel
